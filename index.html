OUR HISTORY

HOW IT ALL BEGAN

Camping, dating back to the 19th century, has evolved into a beloved outdoor activity. Initially an escape from urbanization, it gained popularity through pioneers like Thomas Hiram Holding and organizations like the Boy Scouts. Innovations in camping gear in the mid-20th century further fueled its appeal. Today, camping offers diverse experiences, allowing individuals and families to disconnect, embrace nature, and build a sense of community. Its enduring charm lies in providing a break from modern life while fostering an appreciation for the outdoors and environmental stewardship.
